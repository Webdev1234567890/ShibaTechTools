    ABOUT

    What are Shiba Tech Tools?

    Shiba Tech Tools is an ecosystem of tools, traders, and data on Shibarium; Utilizing scanners and a strong network of traders to gather data and statistics about tokens on the Shibarium Network, and putting it into the hands of our users. $SHIBTECH will offer a large variety of trading tools, gathering data and monitoring specific activity on any Shibarium Token, and active wallets. Over time, as information is gathereed and parameters are met that have shown previous success in other tokens, traders will be able to use $SHIBTECH to assist their conviction of a coin's success.

    What is ShibaTech building?

    All of the data gathered by ShibaTech will be stored on our network and grow our database. As our sample size increases (which will be controlled by the inputs of ShibTech users), the network becomes stronger. We are monetizing relevant data on the Shibarium Blockchain.

    Features

    Features available after Launch:

    Bot tracks:

    - Buys/sells of tokens (USD Value + token amount)
    - Buyers/sellers of tokens (Wallet value + previous activity)
    - Activity of top 1–100 token holders (Categorized into smaller groups, Buying/selling/holding)

    Processes data
    and summarizes:

    - Profit/loss of buyers/sellers on X token
    - Average wallet value of top 100 holders of X token
    - Volatility of supply movement; how much supply is in who's hands
    - Whale movement- buy/sell activity of top holders, safety of tokens.

    Using all of this information, ShibaTech can assist users significantly, giving them information that helps in assessing a token's supply control, strength, holders, and more information which is crucial to traders.
    ShibaTech aims to create a self-sustaining ecosystem, offering traders a solid database and concise, summarized token statistics that will benefit them significantly whilst trading on the Shibarium Network.
